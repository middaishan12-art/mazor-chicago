Our Story

Rooted in tradition.
Made for Chicago.

Mazor is a neighborhood Mexican kitchen on Milwaukee Avenue in River West. Owner Christian built Mazor on a simple belief: great food is generous food — big portions, fresh ingredients, and a room where everyone feels at home.

Everything on the menu is made from scratch — the tortillas, the salsas, the sauces — because shortcuts have no place in this kitchen.
